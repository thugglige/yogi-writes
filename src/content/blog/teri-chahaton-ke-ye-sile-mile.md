Teri chahaton ke ye sile mile
Khokhle aur dharakte do dil mile
Tumse mile hum yuhi dilon ki dararon ke bich mein
Mera ishq itna gahra
Jitna woh samandar do kinaron ke bich mein
Par woh do kinare jaise tu aur main
Ek jung jari hai meri paani kharon se bich mein
Bich hi bich tera khayal iss qadr aata hai
Teri tasveer na dekhu to mujhe kahan sabr aata hai
Aksar aata hai mujhe sapna tera
Aur aata bhi hai to kahan mukhatsar aata hai
