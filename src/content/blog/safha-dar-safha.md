---
title: 'Safha Dar Safha'
description: 'Kahani hai jo teri, hum ta-umar dastan sunayenge — safha dar safha.'
pubDate: '2026-07-18'
category: 'Poem'
tags: ['betrayal', 'romance', 'irony', 'shayari']
---

Kahani hai jo teri
Hum ta-umar dastan sunayenge,
Kaise tune safha dar safha
Kirdar badle hain,
Andaaz se lagta hai
Hum pahle to nahi
Ae nargis bata tune
Kitne didar badle hain.
