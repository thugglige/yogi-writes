When I found out ki ye movies aur web series scripted hoti hai,

Maine khana nahi khaya do din tak.

Ki kya matlab Paresh Rawal ladki dekh ke chhati pe haath nahi ferta.



Aur had to tab ho gayi jab mera saga chacha

Mujhe bolta hai ki Amrish Puri achha aadmi hai.

Maine samjhaya unko ki chacha chup raho, aapko kuchh nahi pata.



Ab imagine karo ek 12 saal ke bache ko —

Uska chacha patak patak ke maar raha hai,

\*"Thoda achha to tumhe lag raha hai, hai na? Haaa."\*



To uss din mujhe pata chala ki duniya gol hai.

Main jaha se pitna start ho raha hu,

Thodi der baad ghum fir ke, fir wahi pit raha hu.



\---



To ab inn sab baato ko kaafi saal bit gaye.

Ek arsa hi bit gaya. Main 15.35 saal ka tha.

Mujhe hostel chhod diya gaya.



Hostel sahi tha. Waha hum 3 jaanwar ek room me rahte the —

Main, Raman aur Raghav.



Aur ek woh aata tha, baade ka rakhwala — Vinesh Dutt.

Jab uska mood kharab hota tha, pit ke chala jata tha.

Ek baar to rasoiye ne chai thandi serve kardi —

Woh aakar hamme pit ke chala gaya.



Vinesh Dutt was a man of principle.

Pura time principle ki chat-ta rahta tha.

Badhiya hai, humari class ko to nahi padhata tha.

Actually maine usko kabhi kisi class ko padhate huye dekha hi nahi tha.

Bas woh har time kisi na kisi ko pitte huye hi dikhta tha.



Aur by looks to maashah allah kya dikhta tha.

Usko dekh ke tumko lagega ki iska to pet name bhi kaalu hoga.

Matlab usko dekh ke aisa lagta tha ki

Ye agar kichad me bhi naha ke aayega to hum bata nahi payenge.

Matlab woh na itna kaala tha ki

Uske baal aur maathe ka matching point tum kabhi dhundh nahi sakte.

Matlab woh na itna kaala tha ki

Batman aur Bane dono ko andhere me pit sakta tha, woh bhi bina dikhe.



\*Haaa.... burai karke achha to lagta hai... khair, let's get back to the story.\*



\---



Now let's setup Raman and Raghav.



Kabhi kabhi mujhe lagta tha ye do log ek hi dimag share karte hai —

Issliye nahi kyuki dono ek jaisa sochte the, nahi nahi nahi.

Kyuki dono aadha aadha hi soch pate hai.



Raman Raghav dono judwa bhai the.

Unke baap ko serial killers bahot pasand the,

To issliye unka naam Raman Raghav rakha tha.

Kitni chutiya baat nahi hai ye.

Aur usse bhi badi chutiya baat — mujhe woh dono bhi mentally sick hi lagte the.



\---



To humare paas 3 bed the, teeno ek saath attached the.

Main aur Raman uper sote the.

Raghav ka gadda humne niche daala tha.



Kyuki woh raat ko ghumta tha.

Woh earth ban jata tha dosto —

Woh apne aksh par to ghumta hi tha,

Humare bed ke chaaro taraf bhi chakkar kaat-ta tha.



Uska bhai Raman aur main dono gaand fati mein —

Isko kahi kuchh ho to nahi jata raat ko.

Maine puchha, ghar pe bhi aise hi karta hai kya?

Bola, pata nahi, ghar pe hum saath nahi rahte.



Matlab thode se ajeeb to the ye log.



To ab hum ek raat to so nahi paye.

Par humne socha ki yaar bhai ka ilaaz to karna hi padega —

Ab to mera bhi bhai ban gaya tha na woh.



Humne \*Shhh... Phir Koi Hai\* se lekar \*Hereditary\* tak saari horror movies dekh dali.

Fir hum gangajal lekar aaye

Aur uss par chhidak rahe hai aur bol rahe hai —

\*"The Power of Christ compels you to calm down."\*

Aur ye sab backchodi hum gangajal chhidakte huye kar rahe hai.



Ab uspe paani pada, uski nind thodi kam gahri huyi.

Woh ghumna band kar diya.

Ab humari aur fat gayi —

Ki shit, ye to kaam kar gaya. Matlab bhut to real hai.



Ab humne jaise taise khud ko samjhaya ki abhi so jate hai,

Subah school jake sochenge.



\---



Ab hum log so gaye. Agle din school gaye.

Bahot research kari humne. Bahot logo se puchha —



\*Ki yaar ye pink wali ladki single hai ya iska koi boyfriend hai?\*



Apni saari approach laga kar bhi hum

Bas uska naam hi pata kar paye.

Woh bhi humme pata chala jab teacher attendance le raha tha.

Aur tabhi humara crush chala gaya —

Vijay naam tha bhai uss ladki ka.



First of all to kyu — khatam ho gaye the saare naam?

Ye Monika, Hemlata, Puja, Vijay Laxmi rakh lete yaar. Sirf Vijay?

Vijay Sharma. Aisa lagta hai koi carpenter ka naam hai.



Khair ab hum log pyaar se bahot upar uth chuke the.

Hum teeno ka dil ek saath tut chuka tha,

Kyuki teeno ko Vijay pasand thi.



\---



Ab bas humara ek hi lakshya tha —

Ki ye jo ghumti huyi chakti hum apne saath lekar ghum rahe hai,

Isko roke kaise.



To humne wahi kiya jo koi bhi sane 15.35 saal ka bacha iss situation me karega.

Humne Raghav ko raat ko bed ke paaye se baandh diya.

Very normal, right?



To ab ye khud to ghum hi raha tha, hum bhi iske saath ghum rahe hai.

Ab to hum definitely sure the ki isme pakka koi bhut hai —

Ek 15 saal ke bache mein itni taakat kaha se aayegi yaar.



Par khair, hum apne soldier ko wahi chhod ke apna gadda utha ke

Chhat pe chale gaye sone.



Ab chhat pe humara rasoiya sota tha.



\---



Ab let's talk about rasoiya.



Humara rasoiya bahot hi aged tha. Woh around 80 saal ka hoga.

Woh aadmi itna ajeeb tha ki

Woh pure veg hostel me chicken bana deta tha,

Fir bolta tha aaj to yahi hai, khana hai to khao.



Uska khana khake ya to aapko saari raat nind nahi aayegi,

Ya aap dining table pe hi so jaoge.

Main to kitni baar dukhti huyi gaand lekar utha hu dining table se subah.

Par khair.



Woh main aur Raman hum ek saath so rahe hai — matlab alag alag bed par, of course.

Ab woh 15 saal ke bacho se itni ajeeb ajeeb baate karta tha

Ki humne 10 minute baad hi decide kar liya ki

Bhut is better option than him.



To hum waapas kamre me chale gaye.



Then we made our peace with the bhut.

We just let him do whatever he wanted.

Aur woh jyada kuchh karta bhi nahi tha. Just ghumta hi tha.

Humme lagta tha kitna fuddu bhut hai —

Jab iske paas itni powers hai fir bhi cardio hi karta rahta hai puri raat.



\---



To ye sab bakchodi chalte chalte kuchh time ho gaya.



Padhai me to hum teeno hi bekar the.

Khel khud humare bas ki baat nahi thi.

Hum to subah uthne se pahle hi thak chuke hote the.



To hume laga ki yaar aise to hum gumnaam rah jayenge.

School me pehchaan to banani padegi.



To humne har ek society aur club ke liye audition diye —

Debate Club, Music Club, Dance Club,

Cheerleader Society, Menace 2 Society, Dead Poet Society.

Aur finally hum sab me fail hokar apni haar sweekar kar chuke the.



Ab humme laga ki yaar aise to naam nahi banega.

Agar koi society humme nahi apna rahi,

To kyu na hum khud ki ek society bana le —

Jaha pe humare jaise bache honge,

Jinhe koi nahi le raha.



To hum ussi plan pe raat ko baith ke discuss kar rahe the ki kya karna hai.



Ab kabhi kabhi na, Kaalu aata tha kamro me —

Ki koi bacha agar 10 baje se pahle so gaya aur padhai karta hua nahi mila,

To woh uski gaand tod deta tha.



Raghav bhai uss din pata nahi kaise jaldi so gaye.

Kaalu aaya, usko sote huye ko hi pita.

Usko to pita pita, humme bhi pita kyuki padh to hum bhi nahi rahe the.



Ab jab usne humko pita to Raghav ki nind khul gayi.

Usne chup chap ek kitab nikali aur padhne lag gaya.

Aur uske jaane ke baad bolta hai —

\*"Thank god bach gaya. Ye to badhiya hai ki meri aankh time pe khul gayi."\*



Matlab iss had tak dheet aur moti chamdi ke ho chuke the hum log

Ki hume pata hi nahi chalta tha ki kisi ne hume pita bhi hai.



\---



Par khair, humare samne ek mission tha.



Humne sochna shuru kiya.

Sabse pahla sawal aaya — society ka naam kya rakhenge.



Tabhi inn half brain logo ne itne ghatiya aur low effort suggestions dene start kare —



\*"The Best Society"\*

\*"Rajma Chawal Society"\*

\*"Our Society"\*

\*"Society Society"\*

\*"Dead Poet Society 2.0"\*



Aur club wale names ke bhi suggestions aaye —



\*"Fight Club"\*

\*"Club Club"\*

\*"Our Club"\*

\*"The Best Club"\*

\*"Rajma Chawal Club"\*

\*"Dead Poet Club 1.0"\*



And finally, after using all our brains, we found the perfect name —



\*\*"The Leftovers Society"\*\*



Bhai, matlab 15 + 15 + 15, total 45 saal ke dimaag ka use karke

Humne kuchh to mast sa bana liya tha.



\---



Ab humne posters banaye.

Surprisingly, Raman bahot achha tha poster banana mein — ye uska hidden talent tha.

Usse bhi pata nahi tha.

Humne usko banane ko bola, usne banana start kiya, aur woh achhe ban gaye.



Ab humne hiring start kar di —

Leftovers ki, jinko koi nahi le raha tha.

Humne posters lagwa diye — \*"Audition Started."\*



Ab dikkat ye ho gayi ki humne ye nahi likha ki

Kaha hain audition aur kaun lega audition.



To koi aaya hi nahi.



Par kisi tarah ye baat principal ko pata chal gayi.

Ab usne humme bulaya aur mujhse puchh raha hai —

\*"Tumne lagaye ye posters?"\*



Maine kaha, nahi sir.

Bolta hai, jhuth mat bolo, maine camera me dekha hai.

Maine kaha —

\*"Jab aapne dekh hi liya hai to yaha bula ke mere maze kya le rahe ho."\*



Pata nahi ye joke usko funny nahi laga.

Bola, \*"Kal apne parents ko lekar aana."\*



\---



Ab main uss din ghar chala gaya. Raman Raghav bhi chale gaye.

Ab maine bataya apne papa ko — papa, chalo, school bulaya hai.

Aur unka reaction bhi wahi tha jo aap log soch rahe hai.



To bahot dino baad ghar ki daal aur maar dono khane ko mili.



Ab agle din mere papa gusse me school pahunche.

Do teen thappad to main ghar se nikalne se pahle hi kha chuka tha.

Matlab unhone nahate huye bich me nikal ke mujhe thappad mara hai.

Main samajh gaya tha ki aaj to ant nischit hai.



Ab hum school pahunche. Raman aur Raghav nahi aaye the.



Ab unhone baat chit ki aur mujhe bich me hi bahar bhej diya.



\---



Ab papa bahar aaye aur mujhe galle laga liya.



Meri gaand hi fat gayi —

Kyuki main to full taiyari karke baitha tha ki aaj to gandi kutayi hoyegi.



Ab papa bole, \*"Chal beta ghar. Ab se tu humare saath hi rahega. Sorry tujhe bachpan se humne thoda strictly pala."\*



Mujhe ab lag raha tha ki ye aadmi pagal ho gaya hai. Ye mujhe maar dega ghar jate hi.

Maine literally khud ko explain karna start kiya —

\*"Papa we were just making a joke. Poster pe jo tagline hai woh bhi meri nahi thi, woh to Raman ne likhi thi."\*



Main samjha raha hu par unka reaction ek dum opposite.

Main samajh gaya ki ye aadmi pagal ho gaya hai.

Mera aakhiri samay aa chuka hai.

Mujhe lagta hai ye ghar jane se pahle hi raste me kahi gaad dega mujhe.



Main khud ke decisions regret kar raha hu —

Ki kya matlab tha poster pe ye likhne ka ki

\*"Tumhe apni crush ki kasam humari society join kar lo."\*

Matlab bilkul bhikhari wali giri huyi marketing.



\*"Tum agar Leftover Society me nahi to tum mard nahi."\*



Humme pata bhi nahi ki "mard nahi" matlab kya hota hai.

Bas bachpan me Amrish Puri bolta tha to humne bhi chipka di.



\---



Par pata nahi kyu papa aur principal vagera sab mujhe bole ki chalo hostel.



Waha jate hi uss hostel ki halat dekhi to papa ki to aankhe bhar aayi.

Principal ne to maanne se hi inkar kar diya —

\*"Bhai saab, humare school ka to hostel hi nahi hai."\*



Vinesh Dutt to kahi mila hi nahi.

Raman Raghav aaye hi nahi.



Pata nahi kyu sab ro rahe the.

Mujhe laga — bhai, yahi gaad ke jayenge tujhe. Bas yahi hai teri last memories.



16 saal ka bacha itna bada to ho jata hai ki woh pant me nahi mut-ta.

Par maine uss din mut diya.



Aur jaise hi maine muta —

Saala rasoiya pata nahi kahan se aa gaya.

\*"Aa gaye tum."\*

Saala creep, mut ki awaaz se pehchan gaya mujhe.



Bhai mere papa itne gusse me —

Pahle to uss 80 saal ke rasoiye ko pita.

Meri aur gaand fat rahi ki ab to next number mera hi hai.

Matlab bahot hi maara bhai use. Kutta bana diya uska maar maar ke.



Fir mujhe ghar le gaye.



Aur Raman Raghav to kabhi waapas hi nahi aaye.

Lagta hai unke ghar walo ko samajh aa gaya tha ki koi fayda nahi hai inpe paise waste karne ka.



\---



Fir pata nahi kyu agle din mujhe ek didi ke paas le gaye.



Woh mujhe samjhane lagi ki Raman, Raghav aur Vinesh Dutt exist nahi karte.

Sab meri kalpana hai.



\*"Tumhare papa ne tumhe bahot strictly pala, to tum bas wahi pitayi vagera hi visualize kar paye. Aur tumhare dimag ne cope karne ke liye 2 nakli dost banaye. Tabhi uss hostel me tumhare alawa koi aur nahi tha."\*



Sounds cool na.



Gaand fat gayi thi meri. Par khair, ab main thik hu.

Or at least mujhe to lagta hai ki main thik hu.

Par ab bharosa nahi hota — kya fake hai aur kya real hai.



I hope this audience is real and I am not speaking to an imaginary audience.



I can just hope.

\*— The End —\*
