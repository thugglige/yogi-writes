---
title: 'Ae Samandar'
description: 'Ae samandar aagaya main tujhse ladne — mujhme abhi thode sans baki hain.'
pubDate: '2026-07-18'
category: 'Poem'
tags: ['struggle', 'survival', 'dreams', 'despair', 'philosophical']
---

Kuchh kinare par khade dekhte
Kuchh dub gaye samundar ki bahon mein
Kayi badh mein bah gaye
Kuchh laut gaye gaanvon mein
Lekin aaj bhi woh lad rahe
Jinka swarth baki hai
Ae samandar aagaya main tujhse ladne
Mujhme abhi thode sans baki hain
Kuchh uljh gaye chakrawaton mein
Kuchh khel rahe hain lahron par
Samandar kinare atkheliyan karte kuchh
To kuchh jujh rahe hain nahron par
Jo dub gaye unpar laaj bahut hai mujhe
Lekin jo tair rahe unpar naaz bahut hai mujhe
Abhi aaya tumse ladne
Thahro thoda kaam hai mujhe
Ab pahunche kuchh uss manzar par
Jahan suryast ho raha hai
Sayad koi lahar uthe sagar mein
Bas yahi intezar ho raha hai
Mere khawab aur duniya ke log
Dono kitne ek se hain na
Lagta hai ab dono ka vinash ho raha hai
