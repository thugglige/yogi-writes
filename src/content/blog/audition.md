### Audition

**Auditioner:** Bhaiya! Woh gulab seller ke role ke liye audition yahi ho raha h kya?

**Crew Member:** Ji haan. Aap thodi der wait kijiye. Hall me baithiye, aapko thodi der me andar bulayenge.

*(Auditioner sits down.)*

*(Call for Auditioner No. 5 — our guy.)*

*(He walks into the room.)*

---

**Auditioner:** Hello sir! Good morning. I am Abhinay. This is my resume.

**Director:** It says you already have experience.

**Auditioner:** Yes sir. Main gaanv me Ramleela me hiran banta hu.

**Director:** What!!

**Auditioner:** Sir! Aap ek mauka to dijiye. Main aapko nirash nahi karunga. Maine saari raat jag ke iss "roll" ki practice ki hai.

**Director:** Hmm. Okay. Let's try this. Imagine you're a rose seller and I come to buy a rose for my girlfriend on Valentine's Day. You have to sell me a rose. It's a simple role. You can do it, right?

**Auditioner:** Are sir, gulab to kya, main to aapko poori dukaan bech dunga. Aap kariye start.

**Director:** Okay. Let's start.

---

### Audition Starts

**Director:** Bhaiya! Mujhe ek gulab lena hai. Ye red wala kitne ka hai?

**Abhinay:** Ye laal nahi hai saab. Ye safed hai. Iss par kisi ka khoon laga hai. Isliye laal lag raha hai.

**Director:** Kya???

**Abhinay:** Ye inteqam ka gulab hai sahab.

**Director:** Inteqam ka gulab...

**Abhinay:** Haan saab. Chaliye main aapko flashback me le chalta hu.

**Director:** Nahi bhai. Itna budget nahi hai apna.

**Abhinay:** Achha to suniye saab. Teen saal pehle kuchh gundo ne mere pitaji par jaanleva hamla kar diya aur issi gulab se unka gala kaat dala saab.

**Director:** Achha, to issiliye ye gulab laal hai.

**Abhinay:** Nahi saab. Woh sab dekhkar unhi me se ek gunde ne khoon ki ulti kar di apne leader ke haathon par.

**Director:** Aisa kaunsa gunda hai jo khoon dekhkar ulti karta hai?

**Abhinay:** Intern tha saab. Intern tha woh.

**Director:** Achha, I see. To uss ulti ki wajah se ye gulab laal ho gaya.

**Abhinay:** Nahi saab. Ulti ke baad gunde ne issi gulab se apne haath ponchhe.

**Director:** Oh, so that's why it's red.

**Abhinay:** Nahi saab. Ulti to peele rang ki thi.

**Director:** Are bhai to gulab laal kaise hai?

**Abhinay:** Kuchh nahi saab. Woh Ajay Devgan aaya tha kuchh din pehle. Usne peek maar ke laal kar diya.

**Director:** To tune iss gulab ko inteqam ka gulab kyu bola hai?

**Abhinay:** Woh to apni dukaan ka naam hai na saab. *Inteqam Gulab Wala.*

---

**Director:** What rubbish yaar. Tumne ye mehnat ki hai raat bhar?

**Abhinay:** Nahi sir. Ye to abhi abhi improv kiya hai.

**Director:** Okay listen. Main tujhe ek aur mauka deta hu. Last chance. No stories. No improvisation. Just sell me the damn flower.

**Abhinay:** Okay sir. Ab dekhna aap acting meri.

**Director:** Okay. Let's start.

---

### Audition — Part 2

**Director:** Bhaiya. Gulab hai aapke paas?

**Abhinay:** G-g-g-g gulab? Hmmm. Gulab hai na saab.

**Director:** Kitne rupay ka hai ek?

**Abhinay:** 70 rupay. 70 rupay ka hai ek. Hmmm.

**Director:** Itna mehanga? Aisa kya hai bhai, ek phool hi to hai.

**Abhinay:** *Ek chutki gulab ki keemat tum kya jaano Ramesh Babu! Suhagan ke sir ka taaj hota hai ek chutki gulaab!*

**Director:** What! It doesn't even make sense. Cut! Cut! Cut!

---

**Abhinay:** Kaisi lagi sir meri acting? Aag laga di na?

**Director:** Bahut achhi thi. Main to bolta hu dusron ke saamne mat kar. Nazar lag jayegi.

**Abhinay:** Bas sir, kabhi ghamand nahi kiya khud par.

**Director:** Nahi beta, karo. Ghamand to karo khud par. Hona chahiye tumhe. Par abhi tum ghar jaakar karo. We'll get back to you.

**Abhinay:** Theek hai sir. Thank you sir. Fees ki baat agli meeting me karte hain sir.

**Director:** Haan haan, sure.

---

### A Few Days Later

**Director:** Beta, humare actors ne tumhara audition tape dekha. Woh bol rahe hain ki iski acting ke aage humari acting feeki pad jayegi. Isi wajah se aapko ye role nahi mil payega.

**Abhinay:** To sir, woh hero ka role de dete.

**Director:** Nahi nahi beta. Woh abhi de diya. Ab woh naraz ho jayega. Samjho na.

**Abhinay:** Theek hai sir. Bye.
