Chalo gaanv ki mitti ko chhodkar
Sahar chalte hain,
Chalo iss bhatti par pakkar
Kundan ban nikalte hain
Yahan thodi late hoti hai subah,
Thode late din dhalte hain.
Log bahut hain yahan par ek dusre se aksar
Kam hi milte hain.
Chalo khul ke baat karein ki
Yahan khule maidan kam milte hain
Chalo gaanv ki mitti ko chhodkar
Sahar chalte hain.

Baston mein bhare woh sapno ke reshe,
Sahar mein hi bune jayenge lekin.
Pathar se sona aur sone se kundan
Sehar mein hi ban payenge lekin.
Kya dabav hai aur kya uski dawa
Kya zindagi kaise hai jeena
Dimag ki nasein khol dene wala
Woh sadkon ka dhuan
Yahi sab tumhe samjhayenge lekin
Ki sapne kaise palte hain
Chalo gaanv ki maati ko chhodkar
Sahar chalte hain.
