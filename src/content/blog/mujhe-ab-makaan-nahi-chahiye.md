Zindagi ki daur chal rahi thi aur
Hamare pairo mein chhale ho gaye
Pata nahi kyun itna badlav aata hai
Logo ke ghar safed aur bheetar kale ho gaye
Maine dekha sab kuchh chhut jate huye
Maine dekha use dur jate huye
Log bole nasha kar le... sab thik ho jayega
Aur jab mujhe ghar se nikala ja raha tha
Maine dekha... unhi logo ko muskurate huye

Din bhar ki thakan de do
Koi to mujhe thoda kaam de do
Mujhe bahut bhukh lagi hai
Thoda sa khana aur ek makan de do
Ghar walo ki bhi bahut yaad aa rahi hai
Mujhe koi unka paigam hi de do
Degree hai mere paas do do
Interview ke liye koi mera naam de do

Din ki do roti aur ek bottle paani
Mujhe aaram nahi chahiye
Mujhe footpath par hi rahne do
Mujhe ab makan nahi chahiye
Kar lunga majdoori pet kaat kaat kar
Chhod do mujhe mere haal par
Bas jab main marun to koi ho aag dene wala
Mujhe kisi mehfil mein ab mera naam nahi chahiye
